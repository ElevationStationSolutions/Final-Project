What is Elevation Station Productions?

Elevator Photography

Elevation Station Productions' primary focus is documenting elevators and other forms of building transportation for the world to see. This is global phenomenon called Elevator Photography.

Railfanning

Trainspotters, rejoice! Elevation Station Productions produces some of the highest quality train videos on YouTube.

Innovation

At Elevation Station Productions, extra is the norm. Here you will find a new form of elevator photograpy that makes the hobby entertaining not just for elevator nerds, but for everyone. While others may try to impersonate the Elevation Station way, there is only on true place you will find the perfect balance of modern videography, elevator research, and an educational and fun experience.

Something bigger

Elevation Station Productions promotes standing up for who you are and doing what you love, no matter how weird or normal that may be. Here, the philosophy is "I do me, you do you." If filming elevators makes you happy, film elevators! We are all people here, regardless of skin color, sexual orientation, or mental state.
